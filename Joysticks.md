Using Joysticks with Aiv.Fast2D
-------------------------------

Aiv.Fast2D supports up to 4 jousticks/gamepad and emulates a subset of XBox 360 layout:

* AxisLeft, the left analog axis
* AxisRight, the right analog axis
* Right, right dpad
* Left, left dpad
* Up, up dpad
* Down, down dpad
* A, the a button
* B, the b button
* X, the x button
* Y, the y button
